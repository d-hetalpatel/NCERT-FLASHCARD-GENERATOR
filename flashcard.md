### 📘 BUSINESS ENVIRONMENT — Concept Summary

Here is a concise exam flashcard for BUSINESS ENVIRONMENT:

**Definition:** The term 'business environment' means the sum total of all individuals, institutions and other forces that are outside the control of a business enterprise but that may affect its performance.

**Key Points:**
1. Business environment includes both specific (directly affecting individual enterprises) and general (indirectly affecting many enterprises) forces.
2. The dimensions of business environment include economic, social, technological, political, and legal conditions.
3. Business environment is a source of various resources for running a business.

**Exam Fact:** A business enterprise supplies the environment with its outputs such as goods and services to customers.
